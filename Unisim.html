For computer simulation class we had a team project on simulating trading on Uniswap decentralized exchange with AgentPy. I traced through Uniswap contract to make the functionality of the exchange in python.
We tested 2 different exchanges one with lower fees and lower initial liquidity and another with higher fees and higher initial liquidity. This is an animation of our results over the simulation time, which show the low fee pool dominates. The Left side graphs are the low fee pool, and the right side graphs are the high fee pool. The top graphs are the distributions of liquidity by price range, and the lower graphs are the fees collected by a position with 1 liquidity in that price range. See the report below for more details.
Tested if liquidity provider agents will either use both a large fee liquidity pool and a low fee liquidity pool or if one pool will dominate. Tested if trader agents with a large balance will prefer to use a higher fee liquidity pool over a lower fee liquidity pool.
In our experiment the low fee liquidity pool dominated in both volume of trades and provided liquidity. All traders perfered the low fee pool.
